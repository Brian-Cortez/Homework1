About Me

Hi there,

My name is Brian Cortez-Reyes, and I am currently a student at Georgia State University working toward a future in game development. I have always been interested in technology, creativity, and problem-solving, which is why game development feels like the right path for me. It allows me to combine programming, design, storytelling, and interactive experiences into something people can actually play and enjoy.

Outside of game development, I also have a creative side beyond programming. I have played violin for several years, which has taught me discipline, focus, and the value of consistent practice . Music has helped me understand creativity in a different way, and I believe that experience can help me when designing games, especially when thinking about atmosphere, emotion, and presentation.

As a student, I am still learning and improving, but I am motivated to keep growing . I want to build stronger programming skills, become more confident with game systems, and continue developing projects that show my progress . I know that game development takes time, patience, and practice, but I am willing to keep working toward my goals. My long-term goal is to become someone who can create meaningful, polished, and enjoyable games while continuing to learn from every project I work on .
